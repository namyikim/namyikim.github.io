I read an autobiography of “Linus Torvalds” 20 years ago. The name of book was “JUST FOR FUN”. It was said that the beginning of "Linux" development was just for fun. My thinking about programming has changed. Before that, I was thinking of programming passively as a tool for university homework and company work. However, It changed me. tried to develop programs to help for me such as programs of movie player and automatically power off of my desktop PC. I have became to like programming. And then, I have written articles on “Microsoftware”, which is famous for programming journal in Korea. In 2002, I have decided my way where I go. I was interested in cellular phone development because it was integrated in lots of technologies, Although the device was so small. I hoped to see and touch it. I finished my military duty in 2005 and I came back to my university. I graduated it in 2007 and I could join Samsung electronics.
